Help

Desktop Controls

  • Hover over a skill icon to view its information.
  • Left Click a skill icon to increase its level by 1.
  • Right Click a skill icon to decrease its level by 1.
  • Shift + Left Click a skill icon to maximize a skill's level.
  • Shift + Right Click a skill icon to zero a skill's level.

Mobile Controls

  • Tap a skill icon to view its information.
  • Swipe Right a skill icon to increase its level by 1.
  • Swipe Left a skill icon to decrease its level by 1.
  • Swipe Up a skill icon to maximize a skill's level.
  • Swipe Down a skill icon to zero a skill's level.
